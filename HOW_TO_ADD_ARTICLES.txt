  E-TecSa-K WEBSITE — HOW TO ADD A NEW ARTICLE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1 — Open this file:
  src/data/articles.js

STEP 2 — Copy the template at the bottom of that file
  and paste it inside the articles array (before the
  closing square bracket ]).

STEP 3 — Fill in your details:
  • id          → unique URL slug, e.g. "my-new-article"
  • featured    → true for ONE article only (homepage hero card)
  • emoji       → any emoji for the card thumbnail
  • category    → e.g. "Technical Guide", "Standards & Compliance"
  • title       → your article headline
  • date        → e.g. "May 2026"
  • readTime    → e.g. "5 min read"
  • excerpt     → short summary shown on the card
  • content     → array of paragraphs; use "## Heading" for subheadings

STEP 4 — Save the file. That's it\!
  The article will automatically appear in:
  • The Articles page (with category filter)
  • The homepage preview section
  • Its own full detail page at /articles/your-id
  • The prev/next navigation on other articles

  TO OPEN THE WEBSITE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Open:  dist/index.html  — in any browser (double-click)

  The dist/ folder is the ready-to-use website.
  After you add a new article you'll need Node.js to
  rebuild. If you don't have it, ask Claude to rebuild
  for you after editing articles.js.
